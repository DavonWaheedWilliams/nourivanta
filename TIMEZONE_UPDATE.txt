NouriVolt per-user time-zone update

What changed
- The app detects each viewer's browser time zone through Streamlit.
- Every current-date default uses the viewer's local calendar day.
- Settings now includes a Time zone section.
- Users may follow their device automatically or save a specific IANA time zone.
- The preference is stored in a new user_timezone_preferences database table.
- Existing accounts and saved records remain unchanged.
- Security lockouts and inactivity timing use UTC so travel and time-zone changes do not affect account protection.

Files to replace in GitHub
- app.py
- elite_features.py
- requirements.txt

Keep these existing settings
- Your current Streamlit Secrets, including DATABASE_URL and OPENAI_API_KEY.
- Your existing Neon database or local data/nourivolt.db file.

Deployment
1. Upload or replace the three files above in GitHub.
2. Commit the changes to main.
3. Reboot the Streamlit app.
4. Sign in and open Settings > Time zone.
5. Leave Follow my device automatically selected or save a manual time zone.

The requirements file now uses Streamlit 1.43 or newer because browser time-zone detection was added in Streamlit 1.43.
